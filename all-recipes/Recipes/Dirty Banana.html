Dirty Banana

Source: liquor.com

Ingredients

2 ounces rum

1 ounce banana liqueur

1 ounce coffee liqueur

2 ounces half-and-half (or one ounce heavy cream and one ounce whole milk)

1 banana, slightly overripe, cut into chunks

Garnish: banana chunk, with peel

Directions

Add all ingredients into a blender with a small (6 ounce) scoop of ice and blend until smooth.

Pour into a Collins glass.

Garnish with a banana chunk.
