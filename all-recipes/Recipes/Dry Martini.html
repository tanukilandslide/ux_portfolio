Dry Martini

Cocktails

Source: liquor.com

Ingredients

2 1/2 oz Gin

1/2 oz Dry vermouth

1 dash Orange bitters

Garnish: Lemon twist

Directions

HOW TO MAKE THE DRY MARTINI COCKTAIL

Add all the ingredients into a mixing glass with ice and stir until very cold.

Strain into a chilled cocktail glass.

Garnish with a lemon twist.
