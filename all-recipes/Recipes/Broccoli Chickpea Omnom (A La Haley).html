Broccoli Chickpea Omnom (A La Haley)

Entrees

Ingredients

2 Heads of Broccoli

1 Bulb Garlic

1 Can of Chickpeas

1 Cup Veggie Broth

Salt & Pepper to Taste

Olive Oil

Option: Lemon

Directions

1) Cut broccoli into florets and wash. Place in large roasting pan!

2) Rinse chickpeas and add to pan!

3) Peel All The Garlic, cut off the weird end parts and any spots, and SMASH! Add crushed cloves to pan!

4) Drizzle with olive oil and toss to coat. Add oregano, salt, and pepper, and toss some more. I guess I would use something like 1/4t of salt at this point. If you need more salt at the end, you can add it then. It will depend on how salty your vegetable broth is.

5) Put the oven on 350Â° F. I have no idea how long I leave it - until the broccoli starts looking cooked. 20 minutes? 30? Just keep an eye on it. Take it out every 10 min or so to stir it around.

6) Remove from oven and pour the vegetable broth evenly, stir, and put back in oven.

7) Stir again after 10 minutes, and remove once the broth is absorbed. Taste for salt. Also, sometimes if I have a lemon, I squeeze some lemon juice over it.

8) NOM
