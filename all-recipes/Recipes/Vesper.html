Vesper

Cocktails, Drinks

Ingredients

1 ½ oz London Dry Gin

½ oz Vodka

¼ oz Lilet Blanc or Cocchi Americano

Directions

Stir gin, vodka, and Lilet together with ice. Strain into a martini glass
With a strip of lemon peel, express by folding and squeezing over glass, then rub along the rim.
Garnish with (expressed) lemon peel
