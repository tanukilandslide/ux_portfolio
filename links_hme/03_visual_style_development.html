Retesting Style

Now that I had created a rough design of our homepage, I knew I needed to test it. The cartoon characters were a bit of a jump, so I gave potential users a page of adjectives and showed them the homepage for 5 seconds to get their immediate initial impression. I had them select up to 5 adjectives that they felt described the website. I then had them to browse the site without a time limit and circle as many words as they liked. Tallying each word gave me a quantitative analysis of how my website felt. To get a qualitative understanding, I also asked them several questions as well. The results are as follows:
